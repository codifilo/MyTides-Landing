Accurate Tide Times & Charts

For iPhone, Apple Watch & Mac

Know the tides before you go. Precise, offline tidal predictions for 6,386 stations across the globe — perfect for fishing, sailing, surfing, and coastal adventures.

Track Tides in Real Time

See live water levels and a full tide curve for the day. Instantly know whether the tide is rising or falling so you can plan your fishing trip, boat launch, or beach walk with confidence.

Plan Ahead with Confidence

Browse an entire month of tidal ranges at a glance. Spot the best low-tide days for clamming, the highest tides for kayaking, or the perfect window for your next coastal adventure.

Every Detail at a Glance

Get complete daily tide tables with exact high and low tide times and heights, plus sunrise, sunset, moonrise, moonset, and moon phase data — everything you need in one screen.

6,000+ Tide Stations Worldwide

Search 6,386 tide stations by name, region, or country. Whether you're checking tides in Miami, Sydney, London, or a remote coastal village, your station is just a tap away.

Find Stations Near You

Use the interactive map to discover nearby tide stations with live tide previews. Tap any pin to see current conditions — ideal for exploring new coastlines or finding the closest station on the go.
